台灣的朋友在哪裡?
knn hamburg steak i can make at home

Ingredients

1. Short grain rice
2. 2 × **Johnsonville cheddar pork patties
3. Garlic soy marinated eggs
4. Green chilli
5. Curry paste with vegetables
6. 100 ml evaporated milk
7. 2 eggs
8. Panko bread crumbs
9. Water

Steps

1. Wash rice, cook in rice cooker. Easy life.

2. Mash 2 Johnsonville patties into one ball.

3. Add 1 egg + some panko.

4. Don’t add seasoning (already salty enough).

5. Shape into a steak.

6. Air fry 180°C for 25 min.

Cook the creamy curry

7. Add curry paste to saucepan.

8. Pour 100 ml evaporated milk + some water.

9. Stir and simmer till creamy.

Pedas hijau

10. Torch green chilli over open flame.

11. Pound into a rough mash.

Serve

12. Rice first.

13. Pour curry around it.

14. Top with hamburglah steak.

15. Add green chilli mash.

16. Finish with soy garlic eggs.

17. Done. 

Quick Soy Garlic Eggs

1. Mix 3 tbsp soy sauce + ½ cup water + 1 tbsp sugar + chopped garlic.

2. Soak soft-boiled eggs 4 hours or overnight.
